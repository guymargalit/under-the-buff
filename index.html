WHO ARE WE?

Under The Buff is a student-run Internet comedy show. We produce short episodes and videos targeted to the undergraduate community of the University of Colorado at Boulder.

We have been making videos since the Fall of 2015. The type of content varies from skits to fake movie trailers but we hope that it at least makes one of us laugh.

We focus on writing, filming, and acting. You don't have to be a film major (currently none of us are) and you should just like making funny stuff. Ok. We're done with our spiel.

HOW TO JOIN

We meet twice a week but the days and times change every semester depending on people's schedules. The actual meeting days and times will be emailed once a poll is complete. Email us at underthebuff@colorado.edu if you want to be part of the poll.

After that, just show up to meetings, pitch your ideas, write something, help film on filming dates, act a little, and then watch as we rack in over 10 views! Views may vary.

Also, join our email list so you know what's going on or get notified when the videos go public. But most importantly, sko buffs.
